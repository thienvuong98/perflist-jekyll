Use this form to request a performance list to be created and posted to this site. You need to have a public accessible Google Sheet containing the raw performance list of your event. Use this template to create your sheet (use File/Save A Copy to your drive). Then edit and share your file to all who have the links. Fill in as much info as you have, but the only fields required are Song and Singer. You could edit the sheet later to update the content and it would be sync'ed up to the generated performance list.
